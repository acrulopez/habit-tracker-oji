# Privacy Policy

**App:** Habit Tracker
**Last updated:** 2026-07-08

## Summary

Habit Tracker does **not** have user accounts, and it does **not** send your data to
us or to any third-party servers. Your data lives on your device, and — if you are
signed in to iCloud on iOS — is backed up to **your own private iCloud account** so it
can sync across your devices. We never see it.

## Information We Collect

**None.** Habit Tracker does not collect personally identifiable information, has no
user accounts, and includes no analytics. We (the developer) receive no data from the
app.

## Data Stored on Your Device

To make the app work, it saves the following **locally on your device**:

- The habits you create (their names and emoji).
- Your check-ins and completion history, so the app can show your streaks and progress.
- Your settings (such as selected theme).

This data is removed if you delete the app.

## iCloud Backup (iOS)

On iOS, if you are signed in to iCloud, the app automatically backs up your habits and
completion history to your **private iCloud Key-Value Store** so your data is preserved
and can sync across your Apple devices. This uses your own iCloud account with no login
inside the app.

- The data stays within your personal iCloud account and is governed by
  [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- It is **not** sent to the developer or to any third party.
- You can turn this backup off in the app's settings, in which case your data stays
  only on your device.
- If you are not signed in to iCloud, the app works fully on-device with no backup.

## Data We Share

We do not sell, share, or transfer any of your data to third parties. The only data
transfer is the optional iCloud backup described above, which goes to your own iCloud
account.

## Third-Party Services

Habit Tracker does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
It uses Apple's iCloud for optional backup, as described above.

## Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including
children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted in this repository with a
new "Last updated" date.
